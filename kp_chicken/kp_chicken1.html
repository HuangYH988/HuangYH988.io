Ingredients:

  • 200g chicken breast, diced
  • 1/4 capsicum, diced
  • 50g peanuts
  • 3 to 4 garlic, sliced
  • 6 dried chilies, roughly chopped
  • a dash of Shaoxing wine

Marinade:

  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • A dash of sesame oil
  • A pinch of pepper

Seasoning:

  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • Sesame oil, to taste
